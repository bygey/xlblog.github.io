    • 六级翻译第三篇--中国"龙"

    中国人对龙的理解与西方人不一样.
    The meaning of dragon in Chinese is different from the West.

    Chinese People's understanding of dragon is different from that of Westerners.

    龙是帝王的象征.
    Dragon is the symbol of emperor.

    emperors

    帝王的子孙被称为"龙种",帝王的用品也常常被加上"龙"字,如"龙袍","龙椅"等,连帝王的面色也被称为"龙颜".
    The son of emperors will be called "the son of dragon"; Things of emperors will be uauslly called adding "dragon",for example "dragon clothes" ,"dragon branch" and the face of emperor also called "dragon face".

    The descendants of emperors are called "childern of the dragon". The objects used by emperors are also usually named with the word "dragon", such as "dragon robes", "dragon chairs". Even the face of the emperor is called "dragon face".

    龙也是一种祥瑞(auspicious)之物.
    Dragons are also auspicious.

    因此,人们希望它能留在自己的家里,保佑自己家里一切平安,万事如意.
    So Chinese people want to keep dragon staing in their houses for keeping everyting safe and good luck.


    Therefore, people hope that dragon can stay in their homes to bless family members, thus everyone can be healthy and everything can go well.

    每到舞龙灯(dragon dance)的时节,人们会请人到家里来舞一回.
    Every time the dragon dance day coming, people will intence(invite) a dragon dance team to dance in their houses.

    When it comes to dragon dance time, people will invite performers to perform in their own homes.

    又因龙呈威猛之相,故也有龙舟(dragon boat)驱鬼辟邪的说法.

    The dragon boat as know as can sweep off ghosts and bad luck because the dragon has a strong body.
