    中国新年是中国最重要的传统节日,在中国也被称为春节.
    Chinese new year,which also called Spring Festival in China,is the most important traditional festival.

    新年的庆祝活动从除夕一直延续到元宵节(the Lantern Festival),即从农历(lunar calendar)最后一个月的最后一天至新年第一个月的第十五天.
    (好绝望,活动,延续都不会😖)
    The party of New Year
    New Year celebrations continue from New Year's Eve to the Lantern Festival,suggesting the celebrations runing from the last day of the last month of lunar calendar to the Fifteenth Day of the first month of the New Year.

    各地from place to place欢度春节的习俗custom和传统tradition有很大差异,但通常每个家庭都会在除夕夜团聚get together,一起吃年夜饭.
    Usually every family will get together on New Year's Eve for having New Year's Eve dinner,although it's so different that the customs and traditions of celebrating New Year from place to place.

    为驱get rid of厄运,迎好运,家家户户都会进行大扫除spring cleaning.
    Every family will have a spring cleaning for getting rid of the bad luck and welcome the good luck.

    人们还会在on门上粘贴红色的对联(couplets)对联的主题为健康,发财和好运.
    People also will stick red couplets,which theme is health,wealth and goog luck, on the doors.

    其他的活动还有放鞭炮set off firecracker,发红包give red envelope和探亲relative访友.
    Other activities incloud setting off firecrackers ,giving red envelopes and visting relations and friends.
